Career Guidance

Essential tips from industry experts to help you navigate your tech career journey

How to become a successful developer:

  • Build Projects: Apply what you learn by building real projects and adding them to your portfolio.
  • Consistent Practice: Code daily, even if it's just for 30 minutes.
  • Learn Fundamentals First: Master programming basics before jumping to frameworks.
  • Contribute to Open Source: Great way to gain experience and collaborate.
  • Networking: Connect with other developers and join communities.
  • Prepare for Interviews: Practice DSA and system design problems regularly.
