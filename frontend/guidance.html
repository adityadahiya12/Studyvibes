Placement Stories

Real journeys from campus to corporate

Arjun Mehta
SDE at Infosys | 2024 Batch

I never thought I'd get placed in my third year itself. My journey started when I attended a workshop on competitive programming in my second year. I was struggling with basic problems while others were solving mediums easily.

The turning point was when I stopped comparing myself to others and focused on my own progress.

I created a study plan focusing on data structures first, then algorithms. I solved 300+ problems on LeetCode and participated in every contest. The key was consistency - even during exams, I solved at least one problem daily.

When interview season came, I was ready. The Infosys interview had 3 technical rounds focusing on problem-solving and system design. My preparation paid off and I got the offer!

Neha Gupta
Data Analyst at TCS | 2023 Batch

As a statistics major, I wasn't sure about my tech career prospects. I started learning Python and SQL through online courses, but felt lost about how to apply these skills.

I reached out to seniors through StudyVibes who guided me towards data science. They suggested specific projects I could build to showcase my skills. I created a COVID-19 data analysis dashboard and a stock prediction model.

The projects became talking points in my interviews and helped me stand out from candidates with just coursework.

TCS was impressed with my practical approach and offered me a role in their analytics division. My advice? Build things, even if they're small. Show your thought process and willingness to learn.
